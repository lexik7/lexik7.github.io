Our Design Process


question_answer

1. Strategic Planning

Begin interviews and space evaluation, assess budget feasibility, determine design intent, gather customer ideas, establish goals & style, existing condition documentation.


tips_and_updates

2. Concept Development

Develop initial presentation with space plans, inspiration images, architectural design concepts, color palette & finish recommendations, presentation sketches, drawings & boards.


fact_check

3. Design Development

Finalize space plans, architectural sketches, and design solutions. Develop the final presentation of sketches & images of lighting, plumbing, and furniture. Samples material options & budgets.


perm_data_setting

4. Construction Visions

Plans for construction, ceilings, furniture, and finished. Elevations & details, specifications of all products, materials & finishes. Potential architectural or engineering drawings.


construction

5. Construction Administration

Oversight of construction. Coordination of sub-contractors, vendors, schedules, budgets, inspections, etc. Obtain permits, develop final punch list, and complete final corrections.
