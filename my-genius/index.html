Who is Stray Kids?

Stray Kids (스트레이 키즈) is an 8-member, 4th generation South Korean boy group. Created through the 2017 survival program of the same name, Stray kids debuted under JYP Entertainment on March 25, 2018. Inspired largely by hip-hop and electronica, the group has explored many genres such as EDM, Rock, Lyrical, Rap, Trap and more. With a wildly unique range of concepts to Stray Kids' discography, the group has created their own sound within kpop, described as being a "mala taste genre" or "noise music".
