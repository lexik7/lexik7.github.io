ENHYPEN's Beginning

ENHYPEN (엔하이픈 - abbreviated as "ENHA" or "EN-") is a seven-member, 4th generation South Korean boy group. Created through the 2020 survival show I-LAND, ENHYPEN debuted under the joint HYBE and CJ E&M Entertainment label "BE:LIFT Lab" on Novermber 30, 2020.

ENHYPEN's Fandom

ENHYPEN's fandom name is "ENGENE". ENHYPEN chose the name because their fans "are like 'engines' that let [the group] grow and keep going." They are also ENHYPEN's "gene" and they "and ENHYPEN share the same DNA to connect, discover each other, and grow together."
