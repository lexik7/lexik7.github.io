My Favorite Thing About School

What are my favorite things about Eastside Prep?

As an Eastside Prep junior, school can be pretty difficult and stressful at times. Classes aren't always enjoyable and it can be hard to have fun. But there are some things I love about school that help me look forward to going each day.

  1. Seeing my friends at school each day
  2. Eating lunch and talking with friends
  3. Playing volleyball after school
  4. Listening to music during free periods

I would say volleyball is my favorite among this list. Even though volleyball only lasts one trimester, the friends I make and experiences I gain are among the most important, cherished memories I have.

Visit the Eastside Prep website to learn more about my school!

I also enjoy some classes over others. For example, I tend to find literature more interesting than history, and even though I find math and physics difficult at times, once I understand what is going on, they can both be pretty fun! The same goes for Web and Spanish! I've found that when I grasp a good understanding in each of my classes, that school and learning can be much more fun.
