My Color Palette

In this website, I am using a monochromatic color scheme. I took this photo of a sunset in my neighborhood over summer, and in that moment, I felt happy and peaceful. This color palette that I designed from this inspiration photo centers around the yellow tones of the sunset. With this color scheme, I hope to convey the same emotions of happiness, peace, warmth, and joy. Below is the inspiration sunset picture I used for this website's color scheme.
