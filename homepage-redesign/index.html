BookTree Kirkland

BookTree's goal is to help every book browser find an interesting satisfying new or gently-used book. We host book clubs, offer special author events, workshops and readings. BookTree is a sanctuary for book lovers and a community gathering place.

Experience the smell of a freshly cracked book
Wide variety of books for readers of all ages
