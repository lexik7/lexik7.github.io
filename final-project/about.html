Who Am I?

My Life

My name is Lexi Kelley. I am 16 years old, Japanese American, and I go by she/her pronouns. I was born and raised in Kirkland, Washington, and I joined Eastside Preparatory School's 5th grade in 2016. Since then, I have grown academically and socially, finding community among my classmates and exploring different hobbies. I also hope to travel the world and learn new languages during my college years and beyond.

Education

Throughout my academic career, I have explored a variety of programs. These include biology, chemistry and physics; algebra and calculus; worldwide historical, cultural and literature studies; photography, web design, and other arts; and language studies. I've come to prefer the art, language, and technology departments over STEM classes, as they complement my passion for creativity and curiosity.


Family Life and Independence

I've grown up in a close family of four: my sister, mom, dad, and me. I also have a dog named Shadow and my grandma who lives right across the street. As of two years ago, my sister has moved to her own apartment and I live in two seperate households for each parent. With this, I've learned independence both financially and socially. I've needed to adapt to instability and navigate my way through hardships.

Current Interests

Over the past three years, I have been diving deeper into my life-long passion for music, photography, and film. Recently, I have been exploring the world and mechanics of cinematography, hoping to pursue the field of study in college and as a career in later life. My side hobby and projects surrounding music and social media have been a major focus for me, but these past few months have also been a time for me to spend time with friends and experiencing my last years of true adolescence.
