Who Am I?

My Life

My name is Lexi Kelley. I am 16 years old, Japanese American, and I go by she/her pronouns. I was born and raised in Kirkland, Washington, and I joined Eastside Preparatory School in 2016. Since then, I have grown academically and socially, finding community among my classmates and exploring different hobbies. I also hope to travel the world and learn new languages during my college years and beyond.

Education

Throughout my academic career, I have explored a variety of programs. These include biology, chemistry and physics; algebra and calculus; worldwide historical, cultural and literature studies; photography, web design, and other arts; and language studies. I've come to prefer the art, language, and technology departments over STEM classes. These courses favor my current interests and hopes for future areas of study.


Family Life and Independence

In my close family, I have a sister, mom, dad, grandma, and two dogs. My dogs are my pride and joy, and have been my main focus for my past photography projects. My sister currently lives in California for college, and as of two years ago, I live in two seperate households with each parent. With a relatively new living arrangement, a recently obtained driver's license, and only a few months until I'll turn 17, I am learning independence financially and socially to prepare for college and adult life that is so quickly approaching.

Current Interests

Over the past three years, I have been diving deeper into my life-long passion for music, photography, and film. Recently, I have been exploring the world and mechanics of cinematography, hoping to pursue the field of study in college and as a career in later life.
