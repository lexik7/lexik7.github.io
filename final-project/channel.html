My Channel

My current project is my YouTube channel for sped up songs, which I have been working on since early 2021 as a small side hobby. With my love for music, I found creating and posting the audios quite fun and beneficial.

Why?

I wanted to be able to create my own sped up song audios so that I could make and listen to any sped up song I wanted. Although I created the channel for myself, not expecting anyone to ever see the videos, the channel slowly gained subscribers. Still relatively small, the channel has a following of just over 2.1K subscribers and is steadily growing.



Features

Although I post all types and genres of songs and artists, the channel primarily focuses on Kpop and comfort songs. It also features many playlists for artists, albums, genres, and "vibe" playlists such as compilations of softer, comfort songs; upbeat, dance songs; songs to blast in your car at night; and more like that.

Benefits

I take requests from subscribers, which has been an amazing way to discover new songs and artists that I'd never heard of or listened to before. Since most of my uploads are songs I enjoy, I listen to my own videos more than on any other music platform. The playlists I create are for myself as much as my subscribers, since I categorize songs based on my own tastes. And for me, speeding up a song even the smallest bit often changes it drastically, its shifted pitch giving it a new sound and vibe. With an endless stream of "new" songs, I never bore of listening to music.
