Music

Music In My Life

Music is and always has been a key part of my life. Music is a sort of therapy and safe place for me, and is one of my few true passions in every way. Songs capture and store memories, and the flow of one's music taste scores and tracks the progress of their life and identity. And I adore that. You will rarely catch me without my headphones on or earbuds in.

My Music Tastes

I grew up on classics from the 80s and 90s, andn my dad's love for grunge and rock. I share my grandma's admiration for jazz and classical, especially among cinematic soundtracks. Throughout high school, I've grown to love various genres like R&B, rap, indie, and K-pop music. Artists like Stray Kids and Cigarettes After Sex have helped me through difficult times and brought me closer to my best friends today.
