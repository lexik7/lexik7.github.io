Music

Music In My Life

Music is and always has been a key part of my life. It serves as my source of motivation, comfort, entertainment, and exploration of new artists and styles. Music is a sort of therapy and safe place for me, and is one of the few things I am really passionate about in life. You can often catch me with an earbud in one ear at all times.

My Music Tastes

My music taste has shifted drastically over the years. I've gone through rock and roll, grunge, jazz, pop, classical, rap, r&b, and the list goes on. Recently, I've been into Kpop and indie rock. I love attending concerts and meeting new people there! I've made many good friends from different parts of the U.S. at events and shows, and it feels like I'm finding my people.
